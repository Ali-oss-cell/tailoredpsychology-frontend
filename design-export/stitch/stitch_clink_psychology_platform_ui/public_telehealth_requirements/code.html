Everything you need to know for a seamless, secure, and comfortable online psychology session from the privacy of your own space.

Technical Needs

Internet Connection

A stable broadband connection with at least 1.5 Mbps upload and download speeds is recommended for clear video.

Supported Devices

Desktop, laptop, or tablet preferred. While smartphones work, a larger screen provides a better therapeutic experience.

Camera & Microphone

Ensure your device has a working webcam and microphone. We recommend testing these 5 minutes before your session begins.

Our platform works seamlessly in the latest versions of Google Chrome, Apple Safari, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge.

Preparing for Your Session

1

Find a Private Space

Choose a quiet room where you won't be interrupted. Ensure family members or housemates know you need privacy for the duration of the session.

2

Optimize Lighting & Audio

Sit facing a window or light source so your practitioner can see your expressions clearly. Using headphones or earbuds can greatly improve audio clarity and enhance your privacy.

3

Minimize Distractions

Close unnecessary tabs on your device and put your phone on silent. Treat this time with the same focus you would a traditional in-person visit.
